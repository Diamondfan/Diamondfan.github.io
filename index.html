Bio

I am currently a second-year Ph.D. student in ECE at UCLA, advised by Prof. Abeer Alwan. My research area is speech processing, and automatic speech recognition in particular.
I received my Master's degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in June 2019, advised by Prof. Gang Liu. I also got my Bachelor's degree in Communication Engineering from BUPT in June 2016.
